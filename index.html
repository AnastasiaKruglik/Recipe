How to make pancakes

Ingredients Amount
Flour 100g
Eggs 2
Milk 300ml
Oil 1 tbsp
Sugar 2 sp

Instruction

  1. Step. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp oil, sugar and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Step. Set a medium frying pan and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  3. Step. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.
